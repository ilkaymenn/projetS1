Article 2 // Malala Yousafzai

  • Activist : form of collective commitment to a cause of a moral, religious, social, political, associative or trade union nature, often with a view to protesting against what is perceived as an injustice.
  • Spokesperson : person who speaks on behalf of someone else, an assembly, a group.

  • Pashtun : Iranian people divided into several large tribes and speaking mainly Pashto.

Article 3 // Girl Rising

  • CorrElation : link, reciprocal relationship.

  • Misery : state of extreme poverty, destitution.
  • Socio-economic impetus : movement relating to social problems in their relation to economic problems.

  • Non-profit organisation : non-profit organisation is a grouping of two or more people who decide to pool resources in order to carry out an activity with a primary purpose other than their personal enrichment. The disinterested nature of the activity therefore prohibits the distribution of a profit to the partners.
  • Early pregnancies : concerns pregnant girls whose young age increases the risk of health effects and negative social consequences.
